Our Story

Age of Christ Institute, Non-Profit Organization

This is a non-profit institution,in the central part of Ghana, Nkwantanan to be precise. It was initiated on 18th January,2019. The rational behind it establishment is to support the lease previlege within our community who cannot afford basic education and also support individuals to become self-reliance.
Mr and Mrs Antwi are the founders of this noble institution,we begun this charity work in an uncompleted four bedroom apartment,after three months we relocated to a new location were we started with two children in the community who needed support

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower every student to reach their fullest potential, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.
